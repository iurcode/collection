How did it start?

- My personal history -

"In The End" was the first Linkin Park's song I've listened to, because it was the first song on the Linkin Park (Music Heritage) CD that I've bought in a subway passage. Of course it was an unofficial release, but I remember that there was a hologram on the back, hahah. Since then (2003), the era of my collecting have begun. Linkin Park have become a part of my life.
